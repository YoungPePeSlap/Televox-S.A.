Short History and Company Presentation

SC TELEVOX SA is a joint-stock company with fully private capital, established in **1994**. With its headquarters in Târgu Mureș (101 Gheorghe Doja street) [cite: 3, 4, 5], our company has developed continuously, relying on a stable team of specialists, currently averaging 20 permanent employees[cite: 6].

Experience and Professionalism

The foundation of our success lies in the vast and diverse experience of our team. Over the years, we have consolidated our expertise in complex technical fields, providing integrated solutions for telecommunications, electrical installations, security systems, and IT.

Fields of Activity

Over the years, our portfolio has diversified to offer complete solutions to our clients:

Commitment to Quality

The quality of our services is certified and internationally recognized. Since the year 2000, we have implemented and maintained a quality management system, being certified ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OHSAS 18001 (SYSTEMA certificate, IQNET recognized)[cite: 28, 29].

We offer "turnkey" solutions, extended warranty (12-36 months), and free technical consulting, demonstrating our long-term commitment to our partners[cite: 30].
